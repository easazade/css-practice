Shadows

With CSS you can add shadow to text and to elements. In this chapter you will learn about the following properties:
  • text-shadow
  • box-shadow

text-shadow :

different formats for setting text-shadow 
        text-shadow: horizontal-shadow(px) vertical-shadow(px) blur(px) color;
        text-shadow: 2px 4px;
        text-shadow: 2px 4px black;
        text-shadow: 2px 4px black;
        text-shadow: 2px 4px 5px red;
        text-shadow: 2px 4px 5px red , 4px 3px 5px purple; //multiple shadows
